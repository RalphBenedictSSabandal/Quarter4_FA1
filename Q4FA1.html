Why support Angat Buhay? Angat Pinas, Inc., commonly known as Angat Buhay, is a Filipino non-profit organization that aims to empower Filipinos to become communities of active citizens. It promotes accessible and quality education for Filipinos, ensures access to quality healthcare and proper nutrition, engages in community building, and empowers Filipino arts and cultures. Its mission is to respond to the needs of marginalized communities and its vision is to empower Filipinos in becoming communities of active citizens.
